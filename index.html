Briefly

I am an assistant professor Junior AI Fellow at ENSAE/<a href=‘https://crest.science/>CREST in the Statistics department.

I was previously an AI Junior fellow at Université PSL in the CEREMADE and worked as a Courant instructor at New York University. I did my Ph.D. under the supervision of Pascal Massart and Frédéric Chazal, at Université Paris-Saclay and Inria Saclay.

I am mostly interested in topics at the intersection of statistics and geometry, including statistical optimal transport, geometric inference and topological data analysis.
