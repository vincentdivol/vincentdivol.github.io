Briefly

I am a Junior AI Fellow at Université PSL. I am affiliated with the CEREMADE research center in Université Paris Dauphine - PSL. I previously worked as a Courant instructor and CDS Faculty Fellow at New York University. Before that, I did my Ph.D. under the supervision of Pascal Massart and Frédéric Chazal, at Université Paris-Saclay and Inria Saclay.

.

I am mostly interested in topics at the intersection of statistics and geometry, including statistical optimal transport, geometric inference and topological data analysis.
